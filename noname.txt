DIRECTING MC TO ANOTHER PLANET
- get ramc 
- get arc between ramc and ra of planet
- arc / 0deg59min8.33sec (naibod) = years,decyears
- decyears * 365 = days elapsed
- get corresponding date (add elapsed to birth time)

DIRECTING PLANET TO PLANET
- get difference in OA of the 2 planets (arc)
- arc / 0deg59min8.33sec (naibod) = years,decyears
- decyears * 365 = days elapsed
